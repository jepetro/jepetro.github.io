Home

My hobby is airsoft I've been doing this hobby/sport for about 5 years now. If you don't know anything about airsoft I'll give you the general idea of the sport. Airsoft is something similar to paintball but rather than paint being the projectiles 6mm plastic balls are the projectiles. The sport uses replica like weapons to shoot each other in team vs team play or one for all.

I love the sport I know I didn't explain alot about it here but that's what the other pages are for. Anyways I am greatful for the oportunities the sport has given me and the places I've gone. It's taken me all over NC even to SC, VA, and even all the way out to CA. I am happy to share the sport with other's and would hope that it peaks some people's interest in the sport.

These are the airsoft guns that I use whenever I go out and play airsoft. There are many types of ways of propelling the bbs but both examples here use gas as a propellant. The rifle set up is called an hpa setup which uses high powered gas like a paintball gun but it shoots the 6mm bbs rather than paint. Types of Fields


So there are really two different types of fields the first being Indoor fields which usually are set up in some kind of building or warehouse. On the other hand there is outdoor fields which are somekind of field set up outside or even a game set in the woods. Both have their benefits and downfalls I personally played at outdoor fields for a long time but had wished someone would make an indoor field in NC but their hasn't been one yet.

Gameplay

Airsoft follows some typical team based sport set up in that there is usually two or more teams that are against one another to complete some kind of objective. That is why I consider airsoft to be a sport as you engage in a team based environment. Most common form of gameplay is called team deathmatch where two or more teams play against each other to elimate the other team and win the round or game overall. But there are many other types of gameplay that are sometimes derrived from video games and played out at a field.

A lot of times when you play airsoft there will be events held based around sometype of gameplay which can range from night games to structured games like the event held at ballack a field in virgina that did an event around the meme of storming area 51. Since airsoft can be pretty heavily team based groups of friends will usually end up creating teams and signing up for events to participate in. Some times these events can be competitive events one in particular that me and my team participated is called SpeedQB which you can find more about that on the SpeedQB page.
