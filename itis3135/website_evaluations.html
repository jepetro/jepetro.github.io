Bad Website

This Website is for a car leasing business in the UK.

  • Contrast: The website has terrible contrast there is such a vomit of multiple colors its hard to focus on one part. Words pop out but they pop out everywhere on the page.
  • Repetition: It does a good job sometimes being consistent like the separation of spotlight cars. The rest of the site is a headache to look at as there is so much variation across the page.
  • Alignment: There is very minimal alignment as the page is all over the place. The lists of cars looks really weird since it is trying to sub categories different body styles.
  • Proximity: Some spacing then the graphics go over the dividers basically making them meaningless. The page has so many over lapping pictures and text making it hard to look at.
